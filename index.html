What each option does

  • AltStore — The original and best-known iOS sideloading tool for adding AltSources.
  • SideStore — A fork of AltStore, works mostly on-device (may require Wi‑Fi; cellular can be limited).
  • Feather — Another sideloading tool that supports adding AltSources directly.
  • LiveContainer — Runs iOS apps without installing (bypasses the 3-app limit) and supports AltSources.
